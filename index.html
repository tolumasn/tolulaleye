Drupal support SLA

When something breaks, here’s the clock

Severity is assessed jointly when the request comes in. Response is when you hear from me; the target is when it’s fixed or mitigated.

Critical · Sev 1

Site down, breach, or data-loss risk — response in 1 hr (Enterprise) · 4 business hrs (Growth) · next business day (Essential). Same-day mitigation, updates every 2 hours until resolved.

High · Sev 2

Core feature broken or major performance loss — response in 4 hrs–2 days by tier; target fix in 1–2 business days.

Medium · Sev 3

Minor bug or non-blocking issue — response in 1–2 business days; fixed within 5 business days or the next update cycle.

Low · Sev 4

Cosmetic issue or enhancement request — logged within 2 business days and scheduled into a future update cycle.

Support hours: Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm ET · Enterprise Care includes 24/7 coverage for Sev-1 issues.

FAQ

Questions clients ask

Do I really need monthly maintenance?

Core, modules, browsers, and attack methods never stop changing. Unpatched sites are the ones that get hacked, slow down, and slide in search rankings. Steady care keeps you secure, fast, and current.

Can’t we handle it in-house?

One skipped security update — or one wrong click — can take a site down. Unless you have a Drupal specialist with spare time on staff, it’s safest with someone who lives in Drupal every day.

Do you support sites you didn’t build?

Yes — most care clients arrive with an existing site. Onboarding starts with a full audit, so nothing surprises us later.

What happens to unused hours?

On Growth and Enterprise plans they roll over for up to two months. Anything beyond your allotment bills at the standard hourly rate, and you can upgrade tiers any time.

We’re still on Drupal 7. Is that a problem?

Drupal 7 is past end-of-life, so it needs extra care — and a migration plan. Both are covered: care now, and an upgrade path to Drupal 10/11 when you’re ready.

How fast can we start?

Discovery call this week, free audit right after, onboarding in about a week. Most sites are under care within two weeks.
